===== COMMON ISSUES & SOLUTIONS =====

Issue: Unable to login
Solution: Clear browser cache and cookies, then try again. If problem persists, reset password using "Forgot Password" link.

Issue: Payment failed
Solution: Verify card details, check expiration date, and ensure sufficient funds. Try a different payment method if available.

Issue: Slow performance
Solution: Check internet connection speed, clear browser cache, disable browser extensions, and contact support if issue persists.

===== RESPONSE GUIDELINES =====

1. Always be professional and courteous
2. Acknowledge the customer's concern
3. Provide clear and concise solutions
4. Offer follow-up support if needed
5. Thank the customer for their patience

===== ESCALATION CRITERIA =====

Escalate to human agent if:
- Issue is critical/urgent
- Customer is highly frustrated
- Issue requires specialized expertise
- Multiple failed resolution attempts
- Legal or compliance concerns
